# Chapter 17 — Fundamentals of Asynchronous Programming

> Book: https://doc.rust-lang.org/book/ch17-00-async-await.html

## Summary
Asynchronous Rust — futures, `async`/`await`, concurrency, and streams.

## You will learn
- `async`/`await` syntax
- Futures and polling
- Joining and racing many futures
- Streams
- The traits behind async, and futures vs. tasks vs. threads

## Subchapters
- 17.1 Futures and the Async Syntax
- 17.2 Applying Concurrency with Async
- 17.3 Working With Any Number of Futures
- 17.4 Streams: Futures in Sequence
- 17.5 A Closer Look at the Traits for Async
- 17.6 Futures, Tasks, and Threads
