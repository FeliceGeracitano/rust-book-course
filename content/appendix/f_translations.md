# Appendix F — Translations of the Book

> Book: https://doc.rust-lang.org/book/appendix-06-translation.html

The Rust Book has been translated into many languages by the community. Coverage
and freshness vary by language, since they track the evolving English source.

The maintained, up-to-date list of translations lives in the Book's own
Appendix F (link above) and in the
[book repository](https://github.com/rust-lang/book) issues tagged
*Translations*.

> This course follows the English 2024 edition. For a translated reading of the
> underlying material, use the official list — then come back here for the
> hands-on exercises.
